chatgpt feedback on cd8 clustering and noise

Validity of Clustering with Low CD8 Counts:

If the CD8 counts are very low and noisy, it’s possible they may not represent

real biological CD8+ cells. Low CD8 expression could be due to technical noise

(dropouts), or the cells might be transitioning between states or have incomplete

CD8 expression. Here are some considerations:

Biological Interpretation: If low CD8 expression is biologically plausible

(e.g., transitioning states or immature CD8+ T cells), these cells might

reasonably cluster separately.

Technical Noise: If you suspect the low CD8 counts are due to noise, it may

be appropriate to threshold these cells or treat them as CD8-.

Marker Heterogeneity: CD8 expression alone may not be sufficient to define

a cell type. Consider using other markers (e.g., CD3, CD45RA, CD45RO) alongside

CD8 to get a more comprehensive picture of the cell population.
